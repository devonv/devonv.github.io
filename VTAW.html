VTArtWorks

About

VTArtWorks is an IMLS funded project administered by Virginia Tech Libraries and School of Performing Arts that seeks to construct a new web platform to facilitate communication for artists and others working in community cultural development (alternatively “creative placemaking”). This first phase of the project is devoted to research gathering, planning, and wire framing for a future, fully developed iteration that might fill the communication gap left by the now defunct Community Arts Network (CAN).

I am working on the project as a CMS advisor and UX designer, although I’ve done lots of different things, from helping with user interviews to building demo research websites using Wordpress and Joomla.
