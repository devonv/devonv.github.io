What is this website?

This is an experimental website meant to explore alternative ways of categorizing and understanding quilts in Virginia's New River Valley. While many quilt repositories focus on the visual qualities of pieced quilt tops, this repository contains only stories about the function and use of quilts in Virginia's New River Valley. You won't find any images at all--just stories!

Thanks to: The Appalachian Quilt Project, Virginia Tech Special Collections, Virginia Tech Department of Appalachian Studies

If you have questions, comments, or suggestions, please contact Devon at quilts @ vt.edu.
